Iowa
Liquor Sales

Background

The Iowa Alcoholic Beverages Division issues Class "E" licenses and permits as specified by Iowa law which expire one year from the date of issuance. Prior to licenses expiration, total spirits purchase in Iowa from January 2021 to January 2022 is analysed to see Iowa Liquors Sales.

Project Background

Baverage class "E" sales have increased in Jan 21 and Dec 21 but at the end of January 2022 there was a decrease of 34%. We are asked to provide forecasting to increase sales in the future. And after we traced further it turned out that Post-holiday sales in Jan 22 decline because lack of customer segmentation.

Analysis and Insight

We managed to analysing the sales per quarter and make a customer segmentation.

    From the result shown above, we make some recommendations:
  • We make cashback USD 1 for every most sales products per quarter, with min. Qty purchase of 10% incremental from previous year.
  • We make a customer segmentation and decided the priority segment based on their purchase activity and give recommendation.
